Advanced AI Dermatology

About SkinAI

SkinAI uses deep learning to assist with early detection of dermatological diseases. Early diagnosis can prevent complications and save lives.

Why Skin Health Matters

Over 4 million people globally are diagnosed with skin cancer each year. Early recognition drastically improves recovery success.

How It Works

Upload an image of the lesion. Our AI analyzes texture patterns, border sharpness, color distribution & asymmetry.

Dataset Used

Trained on the HAM10000 dataset containing expert-labeled dermatoscopic images.

Accuracy

Deep CNN classifiers can reach dermatologist-level accuracy when trained properly.

AI Probability Scoring

Results include confidence scores indicating the likelihood of each disease category.

Disclaimer

This tool is not a substitute for medical visits. Always consult a certified dermatologist.

Skin Health & Treatment Articles

Preventing Acne

Use non-comedogenic products, avoid excessive scrubbing, and maintain hydration.

Melanoma Warning Signs

Follow the ABCDE rule: Asymmetry, Borders, Color variation, Diameter & Evolution.

Psoriasis Care

Moisturizers, phototherapy, and biologics provide relief. Avoid triggers.

Eczema Treatment

Barrier creams + steroid therapy help reduce inflammation. Avoid harsh soaps.

Sun Protection

Use SPF 50+, reapply every 2 hours. UV exposure increases cancer risk.

Healthy Skin Diet

Antioxidants, omega-3 and hydration improve elasticity & healing.
