FAQ

Is SkinAI a replacement for a doctor?
No. Our platform assists diagnosis; certified dermatologists verify results.

Where is my medical data stored?
On encrypted, private cloud servers inside India with controlled access.

Can I get a follow-up consultation?
Yes, follow-ups can be booked at a reduced fee if within 14 days.

Are doctors on SkinAI board certified?
Yes, all dermatologists are verified through medical council certificates.

Can I export my reports?
Yes, PDF reports can be downloaded from your patient portal.

Do you accept insurance claims?
Not currently, but insurance integration is under processing.

How long does a typical consultation take?
8–15 minutes, depending on condition complexity.

Can minors book consultations?
Only with guardian approval. Consent is required for 16 and below.

Can international users access SkinAI?
Yes, but prescriptions follow Indian telemedicine guidelines.

How do refunds work?
Full refund if unused within 24 hours or unresolved technical failures.

Are prescriptions legally valid?
All prescriptions follow Telemedicine Practice Guidelines of India.

Do doctors see my previous scan history?
Yes, only with your explicit consent to improve treatment accuracy.

Do you share my data with third parties?
Strict no. We do not sell, rent or disclose data to advertisers.

What if my skin issue worsens rapidly?
Visit your nearest emergency clinic immediately. Online care is not for emergencies.
